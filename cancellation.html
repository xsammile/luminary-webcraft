Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

1. Cancellation Requests

Customers may cancel services by contacting us via email. Cancellations are subject to the project status at the time of request.

2. Refunds

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Starter Plan: Refund within 3 days of payment, provided no major design work has begun.
  • Professional Plan: Refund within 5 days of payment. A 30% processing fee applies if work has begun.
  • Elite Plan: No refunds once project milestones have been agreed upon and work has commenced.

3. Late or Missing Refunds

If you havenot received a refund yet, check your bank account or contact your card provider. If issues persist, contact us.
