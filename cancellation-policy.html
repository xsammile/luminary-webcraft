Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Web Development Services

  • Before Project Start: Full refund of deposit if cancellation occurs before any work has commenced.
  • After Project Start: Refund of unused portion minus the value of work completed to date.
  • After Delivery: No refunds for completed and delivered work.
  • Project Abandonment: Projects inactive for 30+ days may be subject to cancellation with forfeiture of deposit.

Career Document Services

  • Before Research Phase: Full refund if cancelled before research begins.
  • After Research Begins: 75% refund if cancelled during the research phase.
  • After Draft Delivery: 50% refund if cancelled after first draft is delivered.
  • After Final Delivery: No refunds after final documents are delivered.
  • Revision Period: Cancellations not accepted during active revision periods.

Digital Products & Templates

  • Before Download: Full refund if product hasn't been downloaded.
  • After Download: No refunds for digital products once downloaded due to the nature of digital goods.

How to Cancel

All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to:

Email: luminarywebcraft@gmail.com

Subject Line: Cancellation Request - [Your Name/Order Number]

Please include your full name, order number, and reason for cancellation. We will process your request within 3-5 business days.

Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days from approval date. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.
