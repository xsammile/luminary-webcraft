Website Maintenance Retainer Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

1. Scope of Services

Luminary WebCraft agrees to provide the following ongoing services based on your selected plan:

  • Regular Updates: CMS, plugins, and theme updates to ensure security and functionality
  • Security Monitoring: Scheduled security scans and malware detection
  • Backup Management: Regular website backups and disaster recovery planning
  • Performance Monitoring: Uptime monitoring and performance optimization
  • Content Updates: Minor content and image updates (as per selected plan)
  • Monthly Reporting: Performance and security reports (Professional and Elite plans)
  • SEO Maintenance: Regular SEO checks and optimizations (Elite and Enterprise plans)

Additional services beyond the defined scope will be billed separately or may require an upgrade to your retainer plan.

3. Payment Terms

  • Billing Cycle: Fees are payable monthly in advance
  • Payment Methods: EFT, PayFast, or PayPal
  • Due Date: Payments are due on the 1st of each month
  • Late Payments: Services may be paused if payment is more than 14 days overdue
  • Price Changes: We reserve the right to adjust pricing with 30 days notice

4. Term & Termination

  • Contract Term: Month-to-month basis unless annual plan selected
  • Notice Period: 30 days written notice required for termination
  • Pro-rated Refunds: No refunds for partial months of service
  • Service Continuity: All services will continue until the end of the paid period

5. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide timely access to required systems, hosting, and CMS for updates
  • Respond to content update requests and approval requests within 3 business days
  • Maintain active domain and hosting services
  • Provide clear instructions for content updates and changes
  • Notify us of any third-party integrations or changes that may affect the website

6. Service Level Agreement

  • Response Time: Initial response within 24 hours during business hours
  • Update Implementation: Content updates completed within 3-5 business days
  • Emergency Fixes: Critical security issues addressed within 24 hours
  • Business Hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM SAST (excluding public holidays)

7. Limitation of Liability

Luminary WebCraft shall not be held liable for:

  • Data loss, downtime, or damages caused by third-party services
  • Issues resulting from client actions or modifications
  • External attacks, provided industry-standard security measures were in place
  • Consequential or incidental damages arising from service interruptions

Our liability is limited to the amount paid for services during the previous 3-month period.

8. Out of Scope Services

The following services are not included in maintenance retainers and will be billed separately:

  • Major website redesign or restructuring
  • New feature development or custom programming
  • Graphic design or creation of new visual elements
  • Content writing or copy creation
  • SEO strategy development or keyword research
  • Social media management or marketing campaigns

9. Agreement Acceptance

By subscribing to a maintenance plan, the client acknowledges they have read, understood, and agree to all terms outlined in this retainer agreement.

This agreement supersedes any prior verbal or written agreements regarding maintenance services.
