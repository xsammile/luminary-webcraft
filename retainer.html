Website Maintenance & Support Retainer Agreement

Effective Date: July 2025

1. Scope of Services

Luminary WebCraft agrees to provide the following ongoing services:

  • Regular updates to CMS, plugins, and themes
  • Scheduled website backups and security scans
  • Uptime and performance monitoring
  • Minor content and image updates (as per selected plan)
  • Monthly reporting (Professional and Elite plans)
  • SEO checks (Elite and Enterprise plans)

Additional services beyond scope will be billed separately or may require a retainer plan upgrade.

3. Payment Terms

  • Fees are payable monthly in advance
  • Payments are due on the 1st of each month via EFT, Paystack, or PayPal
  • Late payments may result in paused services until resolved

4. Term & Termination

This agreement operates on a month-to-month basis unless an annual plan is selected. Either party may terminate the agreement with 30 days written notice. No refunds will be issued for partial months.

5. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide timely access to required systems for updates
  • Respond to content update requests within a reasonable timeframe

6. Limitation of Liability

Luminary WebCraft shall not be held liable for data loss, downtime, or damages caused by third-party services, client actions, or external attacks, provided that industry-standard security and backups were in place.

7. Agreement Acceptance

By subscribing to a maintenance plan, the client agrees to the terms outlined in this agreement.
