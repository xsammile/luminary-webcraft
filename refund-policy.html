Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Overview

At Luminary WebCraft, we strive to deliver exceptional quality in all our services. We understand that circumstances may change, and we have designed this refund policy to be fair to both our clients and our business.

Refund Eligibility

Web Development Services

  • 100% Refund: Available if cancellation occurs before project kickoff and no work has commenced.
  • Partial Refund: Calculated based on work completed vs. work remaining. Project manager will provide detailed breakdown.
  • No Refund: After project completion and final delivery. This includes bug fixes covered under warranty period.

Career Document Services

  • 100% Refund: Before research phase begins.
  • 75% Refund: During research phase but before draft creation.
  • 50% Refund: After first draft delivery but before final delivery.
  • No Refund: After final document delivery and revision period completion.

Digital Products & Templates

  • 100% Refund: If product hasn't been downloaded within 24 hours of purchase.
  • No Refund: After digital product download due to the nature of digital goods.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Custom domain names and third-party service fees
  • Hosting services already activated
  • SSL certificates and security services
  • Any third-party software licenses purchased on client's behalf
  • Services marked as "non-refundable" at time of purchase

Refund Processing

  • Processing Time: 7-10 business days from approval date
  • Method: Refund will be issued to original payment method
  • Notification: You will receive email confirmation once refund is processed
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days may be required by your financial institution

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us with the following information:

  • Full name and contact information
  • Order number and date of purchase
  • Detailed reason for refund request
  • Any supporting documentation

Email: luminarywebcraft@gmail.com

Response Time: We will respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.

Dispute Resolution

If you are unsatisfied with our refund decision, you may request a review by our management team. We are committed to finding fair solutions for all parties involved.
